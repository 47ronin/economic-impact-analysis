Building Community as Capacity

The CfA Brigade organizes and supports networks of volunteer “civic hackers” to work with local governments to open data, implement civic software, and use the web as a platform for civic engagement. In times of shrinking budgets and higher demands on government, it's key for the community to act as additional capacity for cities.
