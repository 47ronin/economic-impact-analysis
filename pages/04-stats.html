Code for America started with an idea: a call to service for the tech world

Coders, designers and others from the fast-paced, world-changing technology industry want to make a difference. Leaders in local governments want to innovate. The fellowship pairs small tech teams giving a year of service with cities and counties eager for new approaches, and stewards both through a structured year of building apps, piloting user-centered design and lean startup techniques, and ultimately changing the culture of government. The success of the fellowship is measured not in lines of code, but in sustainable, meaningful change to both institutions and their next generation of leaders.
