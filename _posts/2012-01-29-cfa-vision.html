2013 was a big and busy year.

We tackled harder problems like criminal justice in Louisville and food access in San Francisco. We grew and refined our programs -- and then took them international. We began to tackle the systematic policy problems and really define how together we can transform our communities -- in practice and policy. Hundreds of passionate citizens, many of whom are extremely talented technologists, joined our cause. Now more than ever, people around the world are coding for their communities.
