Peer Network

The Peer Network is where government workers connect to share insights and ideas -- and explore new approaches to government innovation. Through regular trainings, events, and discussions Code for America helps facilitate the transfer of ideas and foster new practices and policies. The program launched at the end of 2012 with and took off in 2013 quickly gaining momentum and membership from civic workers coast-to-coast.
