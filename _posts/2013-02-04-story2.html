Economic Engine

portofsandiego.org
‹ ›


The Port of San Diego is indeed a regional economic engine, supported by a new report that shows the Port had a total economic impact of $7.6 billion on the San Diego region in 2013.







Direct economic output from Port tenants increased by about 8 percent and direct jobs increased by about 9 percent since the last analysis.
