Creating a Global Network

In 2013, in response to massive interest from countries around the world, we launched Code for All to help organizations everywhere implement our model. Through fostering this network of civic change agents, we are building a global movement of more innovative and open governments. Lessons from the Fellowship, Brigade and Peer Network are being cascaded to local governments across the globe.
