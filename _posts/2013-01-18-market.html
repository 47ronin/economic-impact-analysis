Civic Startups

The Civic Startups program offers entrepreneurs advice and monitorship to grow their companies. By cultivating civic startups the program champion a competitive civic tech market that offers high quality, high value tools. Building on the success of the Fellowship and the Accelerator, in 2013 Code for America launch an Incubator to further the work of the Fellows and turn solid civic projects into inspiring new civic companies.
